Sexy igit

Stop using a spreadsheet! This web app finds fair TF2 item trades automatically.

Due out in Summer 2012 »


1. Sign Up

Create an account with us and link it to your Steam username. A few clicks for verification, and you'll immediately be able to see your backpack and enter the database.

2. Make Your Wishlist

Add items to your wishlist at your convenience. Sexy igit will send your wished-for items to the database and immediately look for good trades. We find good pairs of traders automatically through our unique item appraisal system.

3. Start Trading

From there, feel free to contact other players through Steam for the item trade. With us, you don't have to worry about getting scammed or losing out on a deal. We break down the details of the trade and tell you why it's fair.
